Adventures Around this Beautiful Earth, Documented in Photos

A collection of photos taken by me

Posted by Alora on December 11, 2023

Once upon a time, there was a girl named Alora. She really liked taking pictures. Her grandma gave her a cheap digital camera that had enough storage space to hold 20 small images. When she was older, her mom purchased a nice DSLR that Alora frequently stole to do photoshoots with her friends. She bought also an online photography course.

All this to say, Alora likes taking pictures. She has a bad memory and pictures help preserve the happy memories of adventures.

Luckily, phone cameras have become quite good over the years, so all of these were taken on Alora's smartphone (or her parents').

A Picture Is Worth A Thousand Words

I am quite proud of these photos. I love looking at them, and though they do not completely capture the scene as well as being there in person does, it does much better justice than my words alone could.
